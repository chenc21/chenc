Designing and printing a T-shirt

For this assignment, we designed an original T-shirt about Hudson, WRA or Ohio with CorelDraw. For my T-shirt I decided to create a relatively simple design with a silhouette of the clock tower in Hudson inside of an outline of Ohio.

Dye sublimation design

In addition to the shirt, we also had to design something that would use dye sublimation for printing. Dye sublimation is a process in which the printer uses heat to transfer dye onto various different materials such as fabric, card or paper. For this part of the project, I printed a shot of a CPU die onto a fabric pencil case.

UV PRINTING

We also print a design on somthing using UV printing, a process where UV rays is used to dry or cure ink as it is printed. For this part of the project, I printed a concentric circle design onto a popsocket (although the design turned out to be slightly off center).
