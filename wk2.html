Creating a laser cut laptop stand

In this assignment, I followed a tutorial to make a laser cut laptop stand in Fusion 360. To do this, I created 5 seperate parts - 2 legs, supports and then top and used joints to connect them together. The idea was that I would have the 2 legs holding up a rectangular plate for the laptop and have 2 horizontal support bars running underneath to prop up the legs and hold the entire stand in place.
