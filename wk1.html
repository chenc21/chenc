Newspaper tower

Our first project was, using only sheets of newspaper and tape, to build the tallest tower that could also withstand airflow from a fan. Our group decided to build a tripod base for the tower and add as many rolls of newspaper we could on top of the base. We managed to get a stable tower with 3 rolls of newspaper above the tripod base.
